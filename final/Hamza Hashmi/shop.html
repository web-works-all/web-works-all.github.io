Markets In India

India is a shopper’s paradise. It’s local markets are a treasure trove of finds you will be excited to own. From clothes to fashion jewelry, you will find everything under the sun at one of these sprawling markets. Lajpat Nagar is where you should go for rich handlooms, curios, footwear and affordable clothing. South Extension market is home to upscale showrooms carrying, expensive jewelry, chain stores, luxury items and handlooms. Karol Bagh is a haunt for festive wear. Brides are often seen here, buying their trousseau.

Malls In India

A collection of swanky sprawling malls are popping up on the map, offering the locals a vast array of high quality shopping. Every part of Delhi has one mall or the other but fashionistas target South Delhi for a more update collection. Select Citywalk Mall in Saket is a favorite shopping spot for Delhiites. An interconnected trio of shopping malls present the locals with an expansive assortment. DLF Promenade Mall has a selection of luxury brands unbeatable by any shopping mall in the city. It’s location in Vasant Kunj is a prominent one.
