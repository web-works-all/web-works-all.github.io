About Us

Offering hassle-free travel experiences to millions of travelers, My Pryatan is one of the leading online marketplaces in India that serves as the best platform for the travelers to connect with country’s top travel agents of various destinations. A successful product of Holiday My Paryatan Pvt. Ltd, My Paryatan thrives each day to empower travelers by delivering the best of both international and domestic vacation deals. With a strong network of about 650+ local experts who serve across 65+ destinations worldwide, My Paryatan has been more than successful in catering to the needs of 10Lac+ travelers by crafting happy and hassle-free travel experiences. Providing some of the best-selling international tour packages and domestic tour holidays, My Paryatan enhances travel experiences by letting the travelers customize their own package as per their needs and interests at the time of booking itself. Be it a backpacker, a leisure traveler, a honeymooner, or a budget traveler, My Paryatan caters to the needs of every type of traveler. Fit for families, friends, couples, solo travelers, and even groups, travelers can find suitable tour packages that can further be tailor-made as per their preferences. From flights to accommodation, local transfers to delicious food, and sightseeing to adventurous activities, let My Paryatan do all the planning for you while you get to embark on a relaxed, laid-back, memorable, and a hassle-free journey. Be it the famous international destinations like Jaipur, National Parks, Banaras, Mumbai, Shimla, Dalhousie, and more or popular domestic escapades like Kerala, Leh-Ladakh, Kashmir, and so on, experience the world of travel by making My Paryatan your ideal travel buddy!
